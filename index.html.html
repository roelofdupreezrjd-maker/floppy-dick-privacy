Privacy Policy — Floppy Dick

Last updated: May 2026

Floppy Dick ("the Game") is a free mobile arcade game. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The Game collects only the following:

  • Username — a nickname you choose yourself. It is stored on your device and submitted to our leaderboard database alongside your high score.
  • Game scores — your best score, linked to your chosen username, stored in Google Firebase Firestore.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal information.

Advertising

The Game displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how AdMob handles data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — stores leaderboard scores. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. AdMob Privacy.

Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are retained indefinitely. You can request removal of your score by contacting us.

Children

This Game is rated for ages 17+ due to suggestive (censored) content and is not directed at children under 13.
